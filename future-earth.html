The Dystopian Earth and the Blight


In the mid-21st century, Earth is plagued by The Blight, a catastrophic plant disease that has decimated most crops, leaving only corn as a viable food source. The atmosphere is slowly becoming unbreathable as oxygen levels drop. Joseph Cooper, a former NASA pilot turned farmer, discovers gravitational anomalies in his daughter Murph's bedroom. These anomalies point to a secret NASA facility led by professor John Brand, who reveals that a Wormhole has apeared near Saturn, placed by advanced beings.



The Lazarus Missions and the Endurance


NASA had previously launched the Lazarus Missions, sending twelve volunteers through the wormhole to evaluate potentially habitable planets orbiting a supermassive black hole named Gargantua. Only three pilots - Miller, Mann, and Edmunds, reported back with promising data.

Cooper is recruited to pilot the Endurance, carrying a crew including Dr. Amelia Brand, Romilly, Doyle, and the versatile tactical robots TARS and CASE( They are robots with A.I that control the operation in the spacecraft by the orders of the team.). The mission operates under two contingencies:

plan A : Solve a complex Gravity Equation to facilitate a mass exodus of Earth's population using massive space stations.

plan B : Establish a colony using 5,000 frozen human embryos to ensure the survival of humans.

To keep the crew alive while travelling from Earth to the Wormhole, they are put to

Cryo Sleep : In this system, the temperature is low which slows down the metabolic process in the human body that helps reserve resources on a long journey and keep them in hibernation till then. Here, almost 2 years.
